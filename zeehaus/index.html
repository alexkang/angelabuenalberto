Zeehaus

Zeehaus is an online real estate startup company based in San Francisco. Their mission is to "help solve the housing crisis, lower transaction costs and simplify real estate investing."

As the graphic designer for Zeehaus I’m tasked with creating graphics, icons and infographics to match the style of their website. I work with the web developer to keep the iconography consistent with the branding.

Iconography

The style they wanted me to incorporate in the graphics are modern and simple to go along with their websites theme. So to show that, I did a dual color scheme for the main icons and for another set I went with lines only. This was to make the icons look minimal yet appealing by having contrast. The icons also have rounded corners to have a modern feel.

An infographic I made for the website can be found under the "Make a difference" section of their home page. The infographic was made to accompany the section “Our Mission” in order to help the viewer understand how the Zeehaus process works through visuals.

Website
